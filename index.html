About me

I am Adarsha Paudyal, an Electronics & Communication Engineer from Nepal, deeply passionate about electronics design, embedded systems, IoT, wireless communication, and modern web development.

I enjoy creating innovative hardware and software solutions that connect the digital and physical worlds. I work with circuit design, microcontrollers, Arduino, IoT devices, and smart communication networks. Alongside hardware, I build modern web applications using React, Next.js, MERN stack, Django, and other frameworks. I love sharing my knowledge through tutorials, technical articles, and open-source projects that help students and professionals learn real-world electronics and coding skills.
